About Us

 Urban Dynamix, LLC. A music production and publishing company, specializing in urban style music on a very exclusive and non-exclusive licensing basis, assisting creative directors and music supervisors acquire and pre-clear the license they need for their media projects also serving marketing and advertising agencies, our niche digital content, sound recordings, scores and cinematic themes, without any 3rd party clearances or rights complications.

Our Mission Statement

 Our mission is to assist our clients with music licensing needs, servicing our best digital audio content for their advertising, marketing and branding campaigns, helping companies reach a diverse urban target demographic and expand their message or product offerings to the right audience.

Why Work With Us

 In the music marketplace, every creative individuals, as it relates to media implementations, from filmmakers, artist, producers, directors to executives all have legitimate concerns and deadlines, we take great care in understanding the people whom we're doing business with, as it pertains to music clearance and licensing, we take great pride in taking their issue into account.
